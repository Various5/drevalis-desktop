What it can do today

Every step of making a video, done for you.

Nothing on this page is "coming soon." Everything below works in the version you can install today. Future plans are in the Roadmap further down.

Step 1 · Writing

Scripts that sound the same across 100 episodes.

Set up your show once — the tone, the characters, the look, your intro and outro — then just type a topic. The AI writes the whole script for you, scene by scene, in the voice of your show.

  • Use any AI. Free ones running on your computer, or paid ones like Claude or ChatGPT. If one is down, the app tries the next automatically.
  • Long videos done right. For 10+ minute videos, the app plans the whole story first (chapter outline), then writes each chapter with the others in mind, so it doesn't drift or repeat.
  • Edit anything. Don't like a scene? Rewrite just that one. Want a punchier opening? Regenerate just the hook. Swap whole chapters in one click.
Step 2 · Voice

Four kinds of voices. A different one for each character. No file uploads.

Mix free voices that run on your computer (hundreds of natural-sounding speakers come built in) with paid ones for studio quality. In the script, just write [Alice] or [Bob] at the start of a line and the right voice picks it up — handy for dialogue or audiobooks with multiple characters.

  • Faster on long videos. The app records several lines at once instead of one at a time.
  • Natural pauses. A short breath when one character finishes, a longer one when someone new starts speaking.
  • Tweak without re-rendering. Speed up the narration or shift the pitch — no need to remake the video.
Step 3 · Visuals

Your look, your style, scene by scene.

Settings ready for vertical (TikTok, Shorts) and widescreen (YouTube long-form) — or import your own image-generator setup. Got more than one computer with a graphics card? The app spreads the work across them. One scene didn't come out right? Re-do just that scene, leave the rest alone.

  • Same look, every time. The app remembers your show's style and applies it to every new scene so the look stays consistent.
  • No restart from zero. If something fails halfway through, finished scenes are kept. Hit retry and it picks up where it left off.
  • Rent a graphics card on demand. No GPU at home? On the Pro plan, rent one by the hour from RunPod, Vast.ai, or Lambda Labs — the app picks the cheapest one for you.
Step 4 · Publish

From "done rendering" to live on YouTube — one click.

Hook up multiple YouTube channels, set different upload days for each one, and the app handles retries if anything goes wrong. The Studio plan also publishes to TikTok, Instagram, Facebook, and X. Or just save the video, thumbnail, and description as files and post them yourself.

  • Plan ahead. Drag videos onto a calendar. Pick which days each channel posts, and at what time — your time zone, automatically.
  • Title, description, tags. Written for you alongside the video, ready for YouTube's search.
  • Thumbnail. Picked from any frame of your video — or upload your own custom one.

Plus the small things most tools forget.

The little details you only notice when you're making videos every day.

Audiobook maker

Paste in a long story or article and get a finished audiobook MP3 — proper chapters, cover art, and optional pictures for each chapter. Different voices for different characters by tagging them in your script.

Word-by-word captions

Each word lights up exactly when it's spoken — the look you see on TikTok and Shorts. Pick a style: YouTube, TikTok, or simple.

Background music

A built-in music library, or let the app generate music from 12 moods (calm, epic, mysterious, etc.). The music automatically gets quieter when someone is speaking, so the narration stays clear.

Multiple videos at once

Make up to 8 videos at the same time. Tell the app whether to do short videos first, long ones first, or just in order.

One-click backup

Save everything — your videos, settings, channels — into a single file. Restore on a new computer, or pull back just the videos. Move machines without losing work.

Your logins stay safe

Your YouTube login, your AI account keys, your platform connections — all encrypted on your own computer. Drevalis can't read them.

Live progress bar

Watch every step finish in real time. If something breaks mid-way, hit retry and it picks up from the broken step — not from the start.

Updates inside the app

New version out? Settings → Updates → Update now. The app handles the rest and shows you live progress.

Connect from your scripts (Studio)

If you write your own automations, the app exposes the same controls the dashboard uses. Lock it down with a password of your choice.

One library for all your files

Upload reference photos, extra clips, your own music, your logo. Same file? It's stored once. Search by tag. Use them anywhere in the app.

Cut clips from a long recording

Drop in a podcast, webinar, or recording. The app transcribes it, finds the best 30–60 second moments, and turns a clip into an episode in one click.

Built-in video editor

Trim, cut, drag scenes around. Add text overlays, tweak captions word by word, draw volume up and down on the music. A lightweight preview lets you scrub through smoothly while you edit.

Same character, same look

Upload a face photo and the app keeps that character looking the same across every scene. Same trick for lighting, color, and mood — pin a reference image and the look stays consistent.

Clone your own voice

Record 30 seconds in your browser, or upload a clip. The app makes a voice that sounds like you, ready to use in any show.

Title & thumbnail check

Before you upload, the app scores your title, description, tags, and thumbnail with a traffic-light. Don't like the title? One click for two AI-suggested alternatives.

Work as a team

Invite collaborators as owner, editor, or viewer. Solo? Skip this entirely — no login required for personal use.

Honest first

What Drevalis doesn't do.

It's cheaper to be honest now than to refund you later. Here's what this app isn't.

It won't check copyright for you

The AI generates whatever you ask it for. If it ends up looking like someone's brand or copyrighted character, that's on you to spot before you publish.

It's not a cloud backup

Your videos live on your computer — we never see them. The app has a "save everything to one file" button, but it's up to you to keep that file somewhere safe.

It's not magic

Good videos still need good ideas, good voices, and good visuals. The app saves you 90% of the mechanical work — picking what's worth making is still up to you.

It doesn't follow platform rules for you

YouTube, TikTok, and Instagram each have their own rules about disclosing AI-made content. You follow them — the app just hands the file to the upload button.

It might not work on weird setups

Tested on regular Mac, Windows, and Linux machines. If you're behind a strict company firewall or running unusual software, support is best-effort.

It stops after you cancel

You get a 7-day grace period — everything keeps working. After that, you can't make new videos or get updates until you renew. Your old videos stay on your disk forever, untouched.

Your stuff stays yours

Scripts, voices, AI models, channel logins — everything's on your computer. Drevalis can't see or change any of it.

Your computer, your cost

One flat price. The work happens on your machine — no charge per video. Need extra power? Rent a cloud GPU only when you actually need it.

Not stuck with us

Don't like the AI we picked? Swap it for another one. The app just glues together open tools — nothing is locked to Drevalis.

Common questions

What kind of computer do I need?

See the table further up the page. The minimum: 16 GB of memory and an NVIDIA graphics card with at least 8 GB of dedicated memory. No graphics card? On the Pro plan you can rent one in the cloud for a few dollars an hour. Long videos run smoother with 16 GB+ on the graphics card.

Do I need to install other software too?

Yes — two free helper apps (one for AI images, one for AI writing) need to run alongside Drevalis. The install guide shows you exactly how. Or, on the Pro plan, you can skip both: point at a cloud rental for the images and use Claude or ChatGPT for the writing — no local graphics card needed.

Can I upgrade or cancel later?

Yes. Go to drevalis.com/account any time. From there you can change plans, switch your payment method, cancel, or download invoices.

What happens if I stop paying?

7-day grace period where everything keeps working. After that, you can't make new videos or get updates until you renew. Old videos stay on your computer forever — nothing is deleted, nothing is sent anywhere.

Does any of my data get sent to you?

No. Scripts, voices, videos, and your channel logins all stay on your computer. The only thing our servers do is check your subscription is active — once a day, just a yes/no. Read the privacy policy.

Is it legal to publish AI-generated videos?

Depends on where you are, where you're posting, and what's in the video. YouTube and TikTok make you disclose AI content that might be mistaken for real. You're responsible for the disclosure and for not copying someone else's work or pretending to be a real person. See our Acceptable Use page for the details.
