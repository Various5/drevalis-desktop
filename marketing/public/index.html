What it does

Every step of making a video, done for you.

Set up your show once, then a new episode is three clicks: pick the show, type the topic, hit Generate.

Step 1 · Writing

Scripts that sound the same across 100 episodes.

Set up your show's tone, characters, and format once. After that, type a topic and the AI writes a full scripted episode in your show's voice — scene by scene, with consistent hooks and pacing.

  • Your choice of AI writer. Free options that run on your own computer, or premium cloud models. If one is unavailable, Drevalis falls back to the next automatically.
  • Long-form stays on track. For 10-minute-plus videos it plans a chapter outline first, then writes each chapter aware of the rest — no drifting halfway through.
  • Edit any scene. Rewrite a single line, regenerate just the hook, or swap whole chapters without re-running anything else.
Step 2 · Voice

Free natural voices, premium studio voices, per-character casting.

Hundreds of natural-sounding voices are built in and free — no uploads, no per-word billing. Want extra polish? Add premium voices alongside them. Tag a line with [Alice] or [Bob] and the matching voice reads it — perfect for dialogue and multi-character audiobooks.

  • Runs on your computer. The free voices need no internet — nothing leaves your machine.
  • Natural pauses. Short breaths between lines, longer ones when a new speaker enters.
  • Pitch & pace. Fine-tune without re-rendering the whole video.
Step 3 · Visuals

Your look, your style, scene by scene.

Drevalis generates your visuals in a style you choose — tuned for vertical (Shorts, TikTok) and widescreen (YouTube). Got more than one graphics card? It spreads the work across them. One scene didn't come out right? Re-run just that scene.

  • Same look, every episode. Style references and character packs keep your visuals consistent.
  • Never lose progress. If something fails partway, finished scenes are kept — hit retry and it resumes where it left off.
  • No graphics card? Rent power. On the Pro plan, the heavy work can run on a rented cloud machine instead.
Step 4 · Publish

From rendered to live on YouTube — one click.

Hook up multiple YouTube channels, schedule on a drag-and-drop calendar, and let the app retry failed uploads automatically. Or just save the MP4, thumbnail, and description to disk and post however you like.

  • Multi-channel. Different upload schedules per channel, in your time zone.
  • Title, description, tags. Generated alongside the video, ready for YouTube search.
  • Thumbnails. Pick any frame, or upload your own.

Honest first

What Drevalis doesn't do.

Cheaper to be honest now than to refund you later.

It won't check copyright for you

The AI generates whatever you ask. If something ends up resembling a copyrighted brand or character, that's on you to catch before publishing.

It's not a cloud backup

Your videos live on your computer — we never see them. The "backup to one file" button is up to you to keep somewhere safe.

It doesn't disclose AI for you

YouTube, TikTok, and Instagram each have their own rules about disclosing AI-generated content. You handle the disclosure; the app just uploads the finished video.

It stops when you cancel

7-day grace period — everything keeps working. After that, no new videos and no updates until you renew. Old videos stay on your disk forever, untouched.

Before you buy

What you should know.

Drevalis runs on your own computer — your files, your control. A few honest things to know first.

You connect a couple of free tools

AI visuals use a free image generator you set up once. The AI writer can be a free app on your computer or a cloud service you bring a key for. Everything else is built into Drevalis — there's nothing else to install or manage.

Your computer sets the speed

A good NVIDIA graphics card (12 GB+ memory) keeps AI visuals fast. No graphics card? Audiobooks and still-image Shorts still work, or rent cloud power on the Pro plan. The app itself is light — the AI tools you use decide the speed.

You're responsible for what you publish

The app makes whatever you ask. Copyright, likeness rights, and each platform's AI-disclosure rules are yours to check before posting. Drevalis just uploads the finished video — it doesn't review the content.

Your data stays on your computer

Scripts, voices, videos, and channel logins never leave your machine. Our servers only check that your subscription is active — once a day, just a yes or no. There's a one-click backup button; keeping that file safe is up to you.

Common questions

What kind of computer do I need?

Windows 10 or 11, 8 GB RAM, any recent processor. AI image and video want an NVIDIA graphics card with 12 GB+ memory, or rented cloud power on the Pro plan. Audiobooks and still-image Shorts run fine without one. See the Download page for full requirements.

Do I need to install other software?

Two free pieces you connect once: an image generator for AI visuals, and an AI writer (a free app on your computer, or a cloud service with your own key). Everything else is built into the installer. The Download page walks you through the setup.

Does any of my data get sent to you?

No. Scripts, voices, videos, and channel credentials stay on your computer. Our servers only check your subscription is active — once a day, just a yes/no. Privacy policy →

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. drevalis.com/account — change plans, switch payment method, cancel, or download invoices. After cancellation everything keeps working for 7 days, then stops issuing updates and new generations.

Is it legal to publish AI-generated videos?

Depends on jurisdiction, platform, and what's in the video. You're responsible for the disclosure and for not impersonating real people or copying others' work. Acceptable Use →
