A standard Windows installer

Installs without admin rights, and everything it needs comes bundled — there's nothing else to set up. Your work is saved in your own user folder, so uninstalling never deletes anything you've made. macOS and Linux aren't supported yet.

Get the installer

What you get when you install

  1. 1The app and everything it needs, installed in one place — about 250 MB.
  2. 2Your videos, settings, and backups, saved in your personal user folder — kept separate from the app so uninstalling never touches your work.
  3. 3A Start-menu shortcut and a tray icon. Close the window and the app keeps working in the tray, so background generation isn't interrupted.
  4. 4That's it — nothing else to install, and nothing left running that you didn't ask for.

System requirements

Minimum

Runs, but slower on big jobs

  • — Windows 10 or 11 (64-bit)
  • — 4 processor cores, 8 GB RAM
  • — 5 GB free space for the app and a few episodes
  • — Graphics card optional — only needed for AI image and video
Recommended

Smooth on Shorts and long-form

  • — 8+ processor cores, 16 GB+ RAM
  • — NVIDIA graphics card with 12 GB+ memory (for AI visuals)
  • — 50 GB+ fast SSD for storing episodes
  • — Or rent cloud power on the Pro plan — then even a laptop is fine

The app itself is light. The heavy lifting is the AI tools you connect — they decide your speed, not Drevalis.

What you'll connect

Two free pieces power the AI. You set them up once and Drevalis remembers them — everything else is built in.

An AI writer

Writes your scripts. Use a free app that runs on your own computer, or a cloud service with your own key — your choice, switchable any time in Settings.

Free options available

An image generator

Creates your AI visuals — free, and on your own computer. Drevalis finds it automatically the first time you launch, and ships ready-made styles you can tweak.

Free · runs on your PC

After you install

  1. 1Launch from the Start menu. The window opens after a few seconds while the app starts up.
  2. 2Paste the license key from your purchase email into the activation screen. It activates instantly and unlocks the app.
  3. 3In Settings, connect your image generator for AI visuals — the app usually finds it for you.
  4. 4In Settings, connect your AI writer and pick a default model.
  5. 5In Settings, pick a default voice — plenty are free and built in.
  6. 6Create your first show and generate an episode.

Updates

One click: Settings → Updates → Check for updates. Drevalis downloads the new version, checks it's genuine, and relaunches itself. No reinstalling, nothing to run by hand.

Prefer to update manually? Download the latest installer from GitHub Releases and run it. Your work is saved separately from the app, so reinstalling never touches anything you've made.

Safe and signed

Every release is digitally signed, and the app checks each update is genuine before installing it — so you always know a download really came from us. Advanced users who want the technical details can find them in our release notes.

Stuck during install?

Email support@drevalis.com and we'll help. If we need logs, the app's Settings → Diagnostics page can save them as a zip for you to attach. One business-day response, usually faster.
