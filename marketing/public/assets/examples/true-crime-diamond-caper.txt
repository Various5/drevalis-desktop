The $10 Million Diamond Caper: One Mistake, One Fingerprint

Series: Shocking True Crime: The Untold Stories

--- Script ---

[Scene 1]
On the night of May 15, 2019, a $10 million diamond necklace vanished from a Paris museum.

[Scene 2]
The thief, known as 'The Phantom,' had a flawless record of 14 successful heists.

[Scene 3]
But this time, a single fingerprint on the necklace's clasp was his undoing.

[Scene 4]
Forensic experts matched the print to a notorious criminal, Jacques Moreau, within hours.

[Scene 5]
Moreau, a master of disguise, had eluded capture for over a decade.

[Scene 6]
His downfall came from a paradox: the more he planned, the more he underestimated a single detail.

[Scene 7]
On June 20, 2019, Moreau was arrested in a high-stakes operation in Lyon.

[Scene 8]
The necklace was recovered, and Moreau faced a 15-year sentence.
