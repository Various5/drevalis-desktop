Pricing questions

Free vs. the live demo — same thing?

Same thing. Free = demo.drevalis.com. Data resets nightly, no real uploads. Use it to decide whether the app is worth paying for.

Why CHF instead of USD or EUR?

The business is registered in Switzerland, so CHF is the base. Stripe converts to your local currency at checkout — you'll see EUR / USD / GBP on the payment page based on your card.

Can I pay with PayPal?

Yes — PayPal works on Creator, Pro, and Studio. Bills in CHF at PayPal's published conversion rate.

What counts as a "machine"?

A distinct physical or virtual computer running the Drevalis stack, identified by a hashed hostname + MAC address. Reinstalling on the same box does not consume an extra seat.

Can I change tiers mid-subscription?

Yes, from the billing portal. Upgrades are prorated immediately; downgrades take effect at the next renewal.

What happens to my videos if I cancel?

Nothing. Episodes live on your disk. Cancellation gives you a 7-day grace window during which generation and upload still work; after that they lock but existing files stay untouched and playable.
